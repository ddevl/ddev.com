## Sponsorship

Prefer to pay by invoice or bank transfer instead of GitHub Sponsors or PayPal? Email us at <a href={SUPPORT_EMAIL_URL}>{SUPPORT_EMAIL}</a> and include:

- Organization / legal name and billing address
- Billing contact email
- Monthly amount or sponsorship tier
- Preferred payment method (ACH, Wise, bank transfer, IBAN, SWIFT, or check)
- Any purchase order, VAT ID, or tax forms (for example a W-9) you need

We'll reply with an invoice and payment details. As a [US 501(c)(3) nonprofit](/blog/501c3), sponsorships to the {ORG_NAME} may be tax-deductible.
